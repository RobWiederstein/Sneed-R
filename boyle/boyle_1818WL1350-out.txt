(Cite as: 1 A.K.Marsh. 258,  8 Ky. 258 (Ky.),  1818 WL 1350 (Ky.)) Court of Appeals of Kentucky. Prewit v. Smith & al. Apr. 10, 1818. *1 On the 4th day of the last September term of the Fayette circuit court, Prewit prayed an appeal from a judgment obtained against him by Smith and al. That court continued in session until some time after the commencement of the last term of the court of appeals, though the appeal was prayed prior to the first day of the last October term of this court. Gen. Hardin moved to dismiss the appeal, because the record had not been filed in time.
