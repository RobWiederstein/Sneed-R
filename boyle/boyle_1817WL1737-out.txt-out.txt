(Cite as: 1 A.K.Marsh. 5,  8 Ky. 5 (Ky.),  1817 WL 1737 (Ky.)) C Court of Appeals of Kentucky. John Shannon v. John Dickenson et al. Oct. 13, 1817. CHIEF JUSTICE BOYLE DELIVERED THE OPINION OF THE COURT: *1 This is an appeal from a judgment for the defendants, in an action of ejectment. The points involved in this case, are substantially the same as those decided in the cases of Fox v. Hinton, 4th Bibb, 559, Thomas v. Havans, Ib. 563, and Shannon v. Kenny, decided at this term; and for the reasons stated in the opinions delivered in those cases, we deem the decision in the court below correct. Judgment affirmed, with costs. Hardin and Bibb, for appellant, Pope for appellee.
