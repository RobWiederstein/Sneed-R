CHIEF JUSTICE BOYLE DELIVERED THE OPINION OF THE COURT: This is an appeal from a judgment for the plaintiff, in an action of ejectment. The only error assigned, questions the sufficiency of the declaration, whether the objection taken to the declaration would have been availing, if it had been made at the proper time, need not be determined, for that issue joined, no advantage can be taken of any defect, either of form or substance, in a declaration in an action of ejectment.FNa FNa. 1 Littell, pa. 500. After issue joined on the title in ejectment, no exception can be taken to the form of the declaration. Judgment affirmed, with costs. Bibb, for appellent, Talbot, for appellee.
